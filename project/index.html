My Projects

Here are some of the projects that I've been involved with over the years:

Senior Design Project

As part of our senior design project, my team and I worked with the National Kidney Foundation of Illinois (NKFI) to identify high-need locations that would most benefit from their services. Through the collection and analysis of publicly available health data, we were able to create a dynamic predictive model and put together an implementation plan to help better inform their future decisions.

ISBE: Co-Director of Arch

The Institute for Student Business Education (ISBE) is the largest undergraduate business group at Northwestern. As Co-Director of Arch, the entrepreneurship arm of the larger organization, I actively managed a group of over 40 members and oversaw work on both internal startup initiatives external consulting projects.

Pinoy Show 2019: Head of A/V

Every year, Kaibigan (Northwestern's Filipino Club) puts on a cultural show that showcases student-produced skits, videos, and traditional dances. For this year's Avengers-themed performance, I was in charge of all audiovisual aspects of the show: managing the scheduling, filming, and editing of pre-shot videos, controlling the sights and sounds at each step, and coordinating the backstage on the day-of.
